Privacy Policy for Arc Helper

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Arc Helper ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use.

Data Collection

Arc Helper does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The app:

  • Does not require account creation or login
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Does not access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, or location
  • Does not track your usage patterns or behavior
  • Does not store any data on external servers
  • Functions completely offline

Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device only and includes:

  • User preferences and app settings
  • Favorited items or bookmarks (if feature added)
  • No personal or identifying information is ever stored

Third-Party Services

Arc Helper does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app is completely ad-free and does not collect any usage data.

Children's Privacy

Arc Helper does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not contain any features that would knowingly collect data from children.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Security

Since Arc Helper does not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk. All app data remains on your local device and is protected by your device's security features.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Export

All app data can be removed by uninstalling the application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be:

  • Posted on this page
  • Noted with a new "Last updated" date
  • Announced within the app (if significant changes)

Your continued use of Arc Helper after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
